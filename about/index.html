We moved to Massachusetts after having lived in both the Southwest and California with the desire to run a small farm and future animal sanctuary.

Currently, we are the humble servants of many dogs, cats, chickens and ducks.

We are both vegetarians, and we support organizations that promote the wellness and well-being of all animal life. Among these are:

  • Cape Ann Animal Aid
  • Great Dog Rescue New England
  • Sweet Paws Rescue

In addition, we also support local businesses and institutions in the Princeton area such as the following:

  • Central Mass Locavore
  • Elzire’s Acre
  • Encore’s Closet
  • Furry Tails Grooming Salon & Spa
  • Princeton Public Library
  • Simple Sweets by Susan
