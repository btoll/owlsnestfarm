The Princeton Farmers' Market has moved indoors to a new location, 6 Town Hall Drive! We're in the annex directly behind the town hall and next to the library. Stop by on the 1st and 3rd Sundays from 10am - 1pm!

Fun for the entire family!

For more information and current news, please visit Princeton Farmers' Market Group on Facebook.

Also, please see past coverage in the Landmark!
