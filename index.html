The Princeton Farmers' Market is moving outside on the historic common, starting June 4th. Market hours are 10am - 1pm, the 1st and 3rd Sundays through October.

Join us for live music and fun for the entire family, with 15 local vendors!

For more information and current news, please visit Princeton Farmers' Market Group on Facebook.

Also, please see the coverage in the Landmark!
