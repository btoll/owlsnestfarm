January 15th starts the Princeton Farmers' Market Group's indoor Winter market!

Join us at the Congregational Church of Princeton on the 1st and 3rd Sundays of each month through May from 11:30am to 2pm.

More than 10 local vendors will be available each market to satisfy your family's every need!

For more information and current news, please visit Princeton Farmers' Market Group on Facebook.

Also, please see the coverage in the Landmark!

In addition to our fresh produce, we can bake custom-order dog treats for any special needs. Don't hesitate to ask!
